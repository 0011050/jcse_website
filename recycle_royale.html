About Us

We aim to raise awareness about the many environmental problems our
precious planet Earth faces, then educate and inspire communities to join
us in taking action for sustainability!

Many upcoming sustainability events will be hosted soon. This includes
exciting activities like hiking competitions, beach cleanups, gardening
gatherings, recycling conventions and funding charities!
